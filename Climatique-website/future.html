- Our Reality -

Every person, in every country in every continent will be impacted in some shape or form by climate change. There is a climate cataclysm looming, and we are underestimating the severity of the outcome of our actions.We must come together as mankind for the sake of our future.

Our reality is dull, with very little hope remaining for us and future generations to come. We must put wealth and greed aside. Any action on climate change confronts serious ethical issues of fairness and responsibility across individuals, nations, generations, and the rest of nature.

However, all countries, communities and companies will feel the effects of climate change.

- Consequences -

The consequences of unethical practices are significant and are as follows:

  1. Higher average temperature - A warmer climate creates an atmosphere that can collect, retain, and unleash more water, changing weather patterns in such a way that wet areas become wetter and dry areas drier.
  2. Longer-lasting drought - Hotter temperatures increase the rate at which water evaporates from the air, leading to severe and pervasive droughts.
  3. Rising sea levels - Tides, storm surges, and waves can negatively influence the coast. Waves, swell, and surge can destroy shoreline defenses and erode coastlines.
  4. Wildfires - The fires burn and damage vegetation communities, such as rainforests that take hundreds of years to recover. This kills and injures individual plants or animals, which may lead to the extinction of certain species.

- Solutions -

Everyone can help limit climate change. From the way we travel, to the electricity we use and the food we eat, we can make a difference.
