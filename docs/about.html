About us

Project Climatique is a dedicated community of climate enthusiasts, environmentalists, and advocates committed to addressing the challenges posed by climate change. Our mission is to inspire and mobilize individuals, businesses, and communities to take meaningful action towards a sustainable and resilient future.

At the heart of our work is the belief that collective efforts can drive positive change. We focus on raising awareness, implementing impactful initiatives, and fostering a global community dedicated to environmental stewardship.

Starting out

Climate change is a problem that is causing many young people stress, and introducing them to doomerism.The introduction of doomerism is very dangerous especially in regards to young people like us. Me and my colleagues noticed this rising trend and decided to build this organisation so as to get across to young people. The creation of this organisation led us to meet so many young people. We reached out to so many different climate justice activist to gide us towards the right path.

Objective

This website was created to give young people access to information and to encourage direct changes that can be implemented in their daily life as a collective to help in regards to climate change.

Aims

  • Our first goal is to ensure that all our information has been verified and are correct
  • To always listen to diverse group of people that are more by the climate change
  • We encourage young people to do more research on ways to help
  • On this website a section has been designated for donations to people that are currently affected.
  • There are also daily updates to the new things happening due to climate change
