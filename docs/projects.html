Take Initiative

Dealing with the current issues with climate change requires collective effort of civilians.
So here are ten things YOU can do to help!

Stop Using Disopsable Cups

Throwing away the cup after every use isn't exactly the most sustainable.

Protest

Take active part in disagreeing with unethical business practice.

Forcing change is the key for implementing change!

Vote For Our Future

Next time you vote, consider what each political party plans to do about current and future environmental affairs.

Conserve Energy

Be mindful of how much energy you use!

If you notice a light on in a room you're not using, turn it off!

Opt Cars out

Try to utilize public transport as much as possible.

Even better if you decide to take your bicycle or even better to opt for reaching your destination by foot!
